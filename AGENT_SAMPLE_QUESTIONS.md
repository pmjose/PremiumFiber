# PremiumFiber Executive Agent - Sample Questions

Use these questions with the **PremiumFiber Executive Agent** in Snowflake Intelligence to get insights from Operations & Assets data.

---

## Operations Questions

### Billing Reconciliation
- How many orphan lines do we have by partner?
- What is the total MRC impact from billing discrepancies?
- Show me ghost lines breakdown by MasOrange vs Vodafone
- Which billing discrepancies have been outstanding the longest?
- What are the root causes of billing discrepancies?

### Service Inventory
- How many active FTTH lines per partner?
- What is the total MRC from service inventory?
- Show service lines by region in Spain
- What bandwidth tiers are most common?
- Which OLTs have the most services?

### Partner Orders
- How many connect orders are in progress for MasOrange?
- Show all pending cease orders by partner
- What is the billing impact of orders this month?
- Which orders are close to their SLA deadline?

### ML Anomalies
- What reconciliation anomalies were detected this month?
- Show anomalies with confidence > 90%
- What is the MRC impact from detected anomalies?
- Which root causes can be auto-resolved?

### Network Elements
- What is the average OLT utilization?
- Which OLTs are most heavily utilized?
- Show PON port usage by location
- Compare Nokia vs Huawei OLT capacity

---

## Asset Management Questions

### Asset Register
- What is the total net book value of our assets?
- Show depreciation by asset type
- Which assets have the best MRC efficiency?
- How many services are supported per asset?

### Depreciation
- What is our monthly depreciation expense by asset class?
- Which asset class has the highest gross value?
- What is the average age of OLT equipment?

### Failure Predictions (ML)
- Which assets have >70% failure probability?
- What is the total MRC at risk from predicted failures?
- Show recommended actions for high-risk assets
- Which assets should we replace now?

### Billing Anomalies
- How much ghost billing is on decommissioned assets?
- Which failed assets are still linked to billing?
- What actions are needed to fix asset billing anomalies?

### Performance Anomalies
- Which assets have low MRC efficiency?
- Show high-priority performance anomalies
- What are the recommended actions for underperforming assets?

---

## Combined Questions (Cross-Domain)

- What is the total revenue at risk from both billing discrepancies and asset failures?
- Compare MasOrange vs Vodafone on service lines, discrepancies, and orders
- Which regions have the most operational issues?
- What is the relationship between OLT utilization and billing anomalies?
