# CMO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## Snowflake Intelligence Platform - PremiumFiber Peru

---

## Overview

This demo is designed for CMO/Marketing leadership audiences at PremiumFiber Peru, focusing on campaign performance, customer acquisition, competitive positioning, and marketing ROI.

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"Today I'll show you how our Snowflake Intelligence Platform provides instant marketing intelligence - from campaign ROI to competitive analysis to customer acquisition metrics. Let's see how data-driven marketing is fueling PremiumFiber's growth across Peru."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Campaign Performance Overview
> "What were our top performing marketing campaigns in Q4 2024 and their ROI?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Lanzamiento Plan 5000 Mbps: € 850K spend, 2,100 leads, 18.5x ROI
- Verano Sin Instalacion: € 620K spend, 1,450 leads, 15.3x ROI
- Cambiate a PremiumFiber: € 380K spend, 1,200 leads, 21.1x ROI
- Total Q4: € 4.2M spend, 6,850 leads, 12.4x ROI

**Talking Point:** "Instant visibility into which campaigns drive the best results across all channels."

---

### Question 2: Channel Performance
> "Which marketing channels have the best cost per lead and conversion rates?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Facebook Ads: € 45 CPL, 4.2% conversion
- Google Ads: € 62 CPL, 3.8% conversion  
- Instagram: € 38 CPL, 5.1% conversion (best efficiency)
- TikTok: € 52 CPL, 6.2% conversion (highest conversion)
- TV Nacional: € 85 CPL, 2.1% conversion (brand awareness)
- Referidos: € 25 CPL, 12.5% conversion (best quality)

**Talking Point:** "Real-time channel optimization ensures we're investing in what works best for Peru market."

---

### Question 3: Regional Marketing Performance
> "How are our marketing campaigns performing across different Peru regions?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Comunidad de Madrid: 65% of leads, € 48 avg CPL
- Barcelona: 12% of leads, € 55 avg CPL, fastest growing
- Cataluña: 8% of leads, € 52 avg CPL
- Sevilla: 5% of leads, € 62 avg CPL, tourism segment
- Other regions: 10% of leads, € 58 avg CPL

**Talking Point:** "Regional insights help us tailor campaigns and allocate budget where growth potential is highest."

---

### Question 4: Competitive Analysis
> "How does our brand awareness compare to Telefónica and Orange? What is our share of voice?"

**Expected Insights:**
- PremiumFiber brand awareness: 45% in Lima (up from 32% YoY)
- Share of voice (digital): 22% vs Telefónica 35%, Orange 30%
- Customer preference (fiber): PremiumFiber 38%, Telefónica 28%, Orange 25%
- NPS advantage: +62 vs industry +35
- Speed perception: PremiumFiber leads in "fastest internet" category

**Talking Point:** "We're winning the perception battle in premium fiber - customers know PremiumFiber means speed."

---

### Question 5: Customer Acquisition Metrics
> "What is our customer acquisition cost and how does it compare to customer lifetime value?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Residential CAC: € 180 (down 15% YoY)
- Enterprise CAC: € 850 (stable)
- Residential LTV: € 2,040 (24-month avg tenure)
- Enterprise LTV: € 16,200 (36-month avg tenure)
- LTV:CAC Residential: 11.3x
- LTV:CAC Enterprise: 19.1x

**Talking Point:** "Strong unit economics prove our marketing investment is driving profitable growth."
